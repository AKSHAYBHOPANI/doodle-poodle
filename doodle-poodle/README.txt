A playful, colourful WooCommerce-ready WordPress theme for Doodle Poodle - India's
first Made-in-India hand-crafted creative play figurine & collectible brand. A proud brand from
Vishvakrit.

== Description ==

Doodle Poodle is a bright, kid-friendly e-commerce theme built for WooCommerce.
The home page is fully composable from the Customizer and includes:

* Animated announcement / ticker bar
* Rainbow-gradient hero with call-to-action buttons
* Feature highlights strip (Reusable, Creative Play, Sensory Fun, Safe & Durable)
* "Shop by Collection" pulling from WooCommerce product categories
* Tabbed product grid (Best Selling / Newly Launched / All Products)
* "We Are Available At" - online & offline store lists
* Customer testimonials
* Latest blog posts
* Instagram / community strip
* Sticky header with mini-cart, mobile drawer nav, and a floating WhatsApp button

It ships with full WooCommerce support: product gallery zoom/lightbox/slider,
styled shop archives, single product, cart and checkout pages.

== Installation ==

1. In WordPress go to **Appearance > Themes > Add New > Upload Theme**.
2. Choose `doodle-poodle.zip` and click **Install Now**, then **Activate**.
3. Install & activate the **WooCommerce** plugin when prompted
   (Plugins > Add New > search "WooCommerce").
4. Go to **Appearance > Customize > Doodle Poodle Options** to set your
   announcement messages, hero content, brand colours, store lists and contact info.
5. Under **Settings > Reading**, set "Your homepage displays" to **A static page**
   and pick a page (the theme's `front-page.php` renders the home sections
   regardless, but this gives you a clean front page).
6. Create your WooCommerce product categories and products - the
   "Shop by Collection" and product grids populate automatically.

== Recommended Plugins ==

* WooCommerce (required for shop features)
* A contact form plugin (e.g. Contact Form 7 / WPForms) for the Contact page

== Customizer Options ==

Appearance > Customize > **Doodle Poodle Options**:

* Announcement Bar - up to 3 rotating messages
* Brand Colors - primary, secondary, accent
* Home: Hero - eyebrow, title, text, two buttons, hero image
* Home: Sections - toggle each home section on/off + product count
* Home: Available At - online stores & offline cities (one per line)
* Contact & Social - phone, email, address, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, WhatsApp
